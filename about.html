¿ WHAT IS THE COLLAGE TYPE ?

The collage type is an independent art project that presents
through the style of digital collages different trends and aesthetics related to Pop Culture, Cinema, and Fashion lifestyle.
Custom digital collages are available to purchase and also open for commissions.
